Gulab Jamun Bliss

A Sweet Symphony for Your Taste Buds

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands (optional) for garnish
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, milk, and baking soda. Knead into a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small, equal-sized balls and roll them between your palms to make smooth balls.
  3. In a deep pan, heat oil over medium heat. Fry the dough balls until they turn golden brown.
  4. In a separate saucepan, make the sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until it reaches a one-string consistency. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands if using.
  5. Immerse the fried dough balls (gulab jamuns) in the sugar syrup and let them soak for a few hours, allowing them to absorb the sweetness.
  6. Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds before serving.
  7. Serve the Gulab Jamun Bliss either warm or at room temperature.
