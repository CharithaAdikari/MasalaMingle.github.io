Paneer Tikka Masala

A Spicy Delight for Your Palate

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt with ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Marinate the paneer cubes in this mixture and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Thread the marinated paneer onto skewers and grill until the edges are slightly charred.
  3. In a pan, heat vegetable oil and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Stir in the grilled paneer and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Pour in the cream, stir well, and let it simmer until the curry reaches the desired consistency.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  8. Enjoy your Paneer Tikka Masala with naan or rice!
