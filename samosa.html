Crispy Vegetable Samosa

A Crunchy Delight for Your Palate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, potatoes), finely chopped
  • 1 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring roll or samosa wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger and green chili, and sauté for a minute.
  3. Throw in the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender.
  4. Season with coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Allow the mixture to cool, then fill the samosa wrappers with the prepared mixture.
  6. Seal the edges of the wrappers with water to form triangular shapes.
  7. Deep fry the samosas until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove from oil and place on a paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  9. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.

Enjoy your delicious Crispy Vegetable Samosas!
